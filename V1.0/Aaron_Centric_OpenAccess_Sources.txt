### **Repositories**
1. **arXiv**: Free repository for preprints in physics, mathematics, and computer science.

2. **PubMed Central**: Free access to biomedical and life sciences literature.
   - Access: https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/
3. **CORE**: Open-access aggregator for research papers.
   - Access: https://core.ac.uk/
4. **DOAJ**: Directory of Open Access Journals.
   - Access: https://doaj.org/
5. **Semantic Scholar (Open Resources)**: Aggregated metadata and abstracts.
   - Access: https://www.semanticscholar.org/
